Since 2017

Built by demand, shaped by our clients.

RGML Entertainment started out in 2017 as an event production business specializing in DJ Entertainment. Throughout the years, our team adjusted to the demand of our clients to add Photo Booths, Brand Activations, Rental Services, and more. Today, we operate as a fully-functioning live event production company.

Our unique offerings, such as our brand Grin + Bear Booth, ensure that every moment is captured with style and creativity. We understand the importance of every detail and work closely with you to tailor our services to your specific needs, ensuring seamless entertainment that enhances your event.
